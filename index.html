Zappa in His Element

Zappa was an eccentric musician with a knack for making music that sounds
like it was made during the peak of an LSD binge,
but suprisingly he was straightedge! Crazy, I know.
In addition to being a sober icon he was an insanely
talented guitarist. His ability to name songs is unparalleled
with such classics as "Don't Eat the Yellow Snow" and "Why Does
it Hurt When I Pee?"" (But in all seriousness these songs are important
for the progressive rock movement.)

He was apart of a band called the Mothers of Invention and
they were as noncomformist as one could possibly imagine
from their album art to Zappa's eccentric appearance
everything about them screamed "we will piss off your parents."
their mission was to essentially satirize as much of American
society and culture as possible. They based an entire album
on riduculing The Beatles and the hippie movement they endorsed,
famously titled "We're Only In It For the Money."
